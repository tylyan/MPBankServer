To connect to either our shared memory or memory mapped bank, the first step is to
Please make sure to change the KEY_PATHNAME to a file that is accessible by you
in order for ftok() to work. After that, you can "Make", then the executables
will be created. From there, you can try any of the test cases mentioned in
bank-testcases.txt . For convenience, incase you do not want to open another file,
I have copied the entire file here.
